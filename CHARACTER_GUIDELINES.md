# Yori Character Guidelines

*[日本語](CHARACTER_GUIDELINES.ja.md)*

Yori is the character bundled with Yorishiro. These guidelines describe what you can do with the Yori character. They exist to welcome fan works, not to restrict them.

## Rights

Character design and VRM model by LUCAS ([@lucas_VTuber](https://x.com/lucas_VTuber)). Copyright in the model has been transferred to the Yorishiro project; moral rights remain with LUCAS. See [`CREDITS.md`](CREDITS.md) for details.

The Yori character and its VRM model are **not** covered by the repository's MIT license. The MIT license applies to source code only.

## You are welcome to (no permission needed)

- **Create fan art and fan works** of Yori — illustrations, comics, stories, and other derivative fan creations.
- **Post screenshots, clips, videos, and streams** of Yorishiro with Yori in them — including on monetized platforms (ad-supported YouTube/Twitch channels, memberships, and similar).
- **Use Yorishiro with Yori in commercial contexts** — at work, in your office, on stream.
- **Use screenshots and clips in articles, talks, and educational material.**

Credit is optional but appreciated: `Yori © Yorishiro project`.

## Please ask first

Contact us via [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/sktkkoo/Yorishiro/discussions) before:

- **Selling goods or merchandise** featuring Yori (physical or digital).
- **Using Yori as the face or mascot of another product, service, or brand.**
- **Extracting, redistributing, or reusing the VRM model itself outside Yorishiro** — re-uploading the model, using it as an avatar on other platforms, or converting it for other runtimes. (This is also prohibited by the license embedded in the model file.)

## Not allowed

- **Sexual or explicit depictions** of Yori (the license embedded in the VRM model also disallows this).
- Content that presents Yori as **officially endorsing** a product, service, or opinion.

## Notes

- These guidelines may be revised as the project grows. The latest version in this repository is authoritative.
- Questions and edge cases: [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/sktkkoo/Yorishiro/discussions).
