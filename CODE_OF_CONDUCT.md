# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Yorishiro community a welcoming and
respectful experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability,
ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality,
personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or other
personal characteristics.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Assume good intent, but take responsibility for the impact of your words.
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully.
- Keep discussions focused on improving Yorishiro and the surrounding ecosystem.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, intimidation, abusive language,
personal attacks, sustained disruption, unwanted sexual attention, publication of
private information without consent, and any conduct that would reasonably make
someone feel unsafe or unwelcome.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Yorishiro project spaces, including GitHub
Issues, Discussions, pull requests if they are accepted in the future, and other
official community channels.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, discussions,
and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. Maintainers may also
temporarily or permanently ban participants whose behavior is inappropriate,
threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the
project maintainers through GitHub. For security vulnerabilities, use
[`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) instead.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
